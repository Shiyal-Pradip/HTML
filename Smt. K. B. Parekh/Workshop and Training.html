Workshop and Training

Smt. K. B. Parekh College of Computer Science, Running under the management of Shri Balvant Parekh Education Trust provides best opportunities to the students of BCA/M.Sc.I.T.

A training workshop is a type of interactive training where participants carry out a number of training activities rather than passively listen to a lecture or presentation. Broadly, two types of workshops exist: A general workshop is put on for a mixed audience, and a closed workshop is tailored towards meeting the training needs of a specific group.

We have a provision of special Workshop or Seminar room having capacity of 200 students. The seminar room is centrally air-conditioned and equipped with latest technologies like speakers, internet connectivity and projector.

Every year we arrange Seminars, workshops, and conferences at regular intervals. So that a student can make the performance and enhance their skill and experience. By having exposure to these practices, students can get prone towards the learning by improving performance rich session.

We have organised workshop on various technology which are not a part of the syllabus, like iOS application development, Android application development, WordPress Technology, and so on.

We also provide training platform to the students with the help of different organization like Mahuva Nagrik Bank, Hanumant Hospital, icici Bank, etc.
